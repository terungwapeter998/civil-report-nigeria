Introduction

The Citizens’ Assembly on Community Engagement (North Central) operates under the Office of the Senior Special Assistant to the President on Community Engagement, Dr Abiodun Essiet. It exists to strengthen dialogue, collaboration, and civic participation across Benue, Kogi, Kwara, Nasarawa, Niger, Plateau, and the Federal Capital Territory.

Objectives

  • Foster meaningful community engagement and dialogue
  • Promote civic participation and development initiatives
  • Provide accurate, timely, and verifiable information
  • Maintain relationships with key stakeholders
  • Support regional and state-level programs

Responsibilities

  • Representation: One member per ward across all regions
  • Volunteer participation in community development
  • Information dissemination to government authorities
  • Stakeholder engagement with local leaders
  • Monthly virtual meetings and mandatory reporting

Governance and Coordination

The Assembly operates under centralized oversight with state-level coordination. Leadership is provided by an appointed or elected Assembly Coordinator, ensuring structured decision-making and operational alignment with national objectives.

Code of Conduct

  • Integrity in all civic actions and reporting
  • Impartiality in community decision-making
  • Respectful engagement with all local stakeholders
  • Strict confidentiality regarding sensitive administrative information

Benefits

  • Enhanced understanding of community and government systems
  • High-level networking opportunities within the North Central region
  • Direct contribution to national infrastructure and social development
